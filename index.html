Kelvin Xu

I am a second year master student in the LISA lab at the Université de Montréal studying machine learning (deep learning in particular) under the supervision of Prof. Yoshua Bengio/ Aaron Courville. I previously did my undergraduate at the University of Toronto where I did my thesis in their machine learning group.

My current research focuses on using using neural networks for sequence-to-sequence/image-to-sequence tasks. But there are a lot of interesting problems in machine learning and I aspire to work on at least a few of them at some point.

I am currently visiting the machine learning group at NYU.
